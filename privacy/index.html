Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

Polyplaces Ltd (“Polyplaces”, “we”, “us”) operates polyplaces.co.uk. We are committed to protecting your personal data and complying with the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.

Questions about this policy can be sent via our contact form.

What data we collect

When you place an order we collect:

  • Name and delivery address
  • Email address
  • Payment details (processed securely by Stripe — we never see your card number)
  • The map area and label you selected for your sculpture

How we use your data

  • To fulfil and deliver your order
  • To send you proof approval and order status emails
  • To respond to enquiries
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data to third parties or use it for marketing. No "without your consent". No "for the benefit of a third party". Never. This is a hardline stance that we will not change at any time. We take great care to minimise the data that we collect and process. Simply put, the less data we see, the less there is to go wrong.

Data retention

Order data is retained for 7 years as required by UK law. You may request deletion of data not subject to a legal retention requirement at any time.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with the ICO. To exercise any of these rights, contact us.

Third-party services

We use Stripe for payment processing and OpenStreetMap / Nominatim for map data. Each service operates under its own privacy policy.
