About Polyplaces

Every place holds a feeling. We turn those places into something you can hold.

Polyplaces is a small studio based in the UK. We design and produce bespoke 3D topographic sculptures of real places — the street where you grew up, the hill you proposed on, the coastline you return to every summer.

Each piece begins with you. Using our online designer, you frame exactly the area you want. We then translate that geographic data into a precise physical relief, sculpted in matte white and finished by hand.

Everything is made to order in our UK studio. We never mass-produce.
