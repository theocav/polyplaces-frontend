# Custom 3D Map Pricing

## Overview
Pricing is based on overall map size, with a simple tiered structure to keep ordering clear and straightforward. All maps are custom-made and priced accordingly.

---

## Frameless Custom Maps

- **Small (up to 200mm)** — £25  
- **Medium (200–270mm)** — £35  
- **Large (270–320mm)** — £45  

---

## Frame Add-On

- **Add a frame** — 
    - Small - +£7
    - A4 - +£10
    - Large - +£13



---

## Shipping

- **Flat rate shipping** — £7 per order  

Shipping cost is fixed regardless of size.

---

## Notes

- All maps are made to order based on your selected location.
- Sizes refer to the longest edge of the map.
- Custom or highly detailed requests may incur additional costs.
- If you're unsure which size to choose, Medium is the most popular option.
